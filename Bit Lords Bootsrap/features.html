1 .Easy navigation


Needs to be easily understood. You also want to make sure they can easily scan your navigation and find what they're looking for, while also making it clear what to expect when they click.

2 .Intuitive design and user experience


Similar to the navigation, users want to be able to figure out what information you have on your site, but they also want to be able to access and engage with it in an intuitive way.

3 .Value propositions


Why should your customer choose you over your competitors? A value proposition is a statement that is the core of your brand and your promise to your customers. It's displayed on your website, either on the homepage, header,footer, or About Us page.

4 .About Us


What are your core beliefs as a brand? Include an about us page where customers can learn about the company's founding, history, and mission statement.Customers should get a strong sense of who you are as a brand, how you operate, and what you believe in.

5 .Immersive visual experience


Sites that tell a strong visual story and create an immersive experience have the opportunity to increase on-site engagement and conversion.59% of people prefer browsing "beautiful and well-designed" sites than basic ones.

6 .Creative ways to display authoritative website content


What is your core marketing message?Whatever it is, make sure your content is clear and concise. Use an active voice engage your readers. Be sure to keep it short and sweet.
